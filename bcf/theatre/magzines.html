Available Magzines

PlayBill

Playbill is the theatre-lover's go-to source for the latest news, interviews, photos, videos and more. News, features, performance listings for Broadway, Off-Broadway, London and Regional theatre.

BoxPRO

In publication since 1920, Boxoffice Pro is the reference publication of theatrical exhibition. Serving as the official publication of the National Association of Theatre Owners, Boxoffice Pro includes expert analysis of industry trends.

American theatre

American Theatre magazine is the nation's only general-circulation magazine devoted to theatre. American Theatre provides theatre news, features, artist interviews, and editorials about theatre in the US and abroad.

Stage

In 2009, Digital Content Manager, Patricia Bradford, brought STAGE Magazine online, moving away from the familiar paper newsletter format started by Holley Webster in 1976. STAGE continues to serve theatres in the Philadelphia region, as well as Florida.

Live Design

Live Design is a magazine for entertainment design industry. It is the must-read creative and technical resource for live entertainment professionals in lighting, sound, staging, and projection. Reports from the field, and industry viewpoint.

Limelight

Limelight magazine provides news, reviews and musings from Australia's premier platform for music, arts and culture. Get to know about the latest updates on theatre at Limelight.

Dramatics

Dramatics is an online magazine for high school students about all things theatre, from practical tips to profiles about Broadway and theatre professionals.

Theatre Times

TheTheatreTimes.com is a non-partisan, global theatre portal. Since its launch in November 2016, TheTheatreTimes.com has published over 4,700 articles covering theatre in 90 countries and regions.

PIE

PIE reviews plays in London, across the UK and around the world as well featuring interviews with directors, actors and technicians. Our contributors write features about theatre history and book reviews.

Theatre News

The BBC of the theatre industry, created for and run by theatre lovers. Theatre News is a leading independent publisher featuring news, reviews, interviews, competitions, the latest releases and gossips..

Intermission

Intermission is an online theatre magazine created by The Company Theatre. Our mandate is to help break down the fourth wall that exists between artists and audiences by providing a platform for artists to talk about the ideas they're grappling with both onstage and off.

TCQ

Since its founding in 1961, TCG's constituency has grown from a handful of groundbreaking theatres to over 700 Member Theatres and affiliate organizations and over 7,000 Individual Members.

Broadway World

Your guide to all things theatre on Broadway and around the world including shows, news, reviews, broadway tickets, regional theatre and more. Broadway World is the leading Web Site for Broadway, Theatre, and Live Entertainment Around the World!

The Stage

The Stage provides news and services for the UK theatre, entertainment and performing arts industry. Established in 1880, The Stage is a private limited company owned by the family of one of the two founders.

WhatsOnStage

Read all of our theatre and performing arts coverage across the U.K., from exclusive interviews and reviews to photos and videos. WhatsOnStage is now the leading theatre website in the UK and the most trusted source for theatre information and editorial content.
